Let me introduce my hobby.

#1.Listening to music

Listening to music is my favorite hobby.

I listen to music whenever I have time, but especially on my way to school. Because listening to music makes me feel less bored.

I like various genres of music and listen to different genres of music depending on the situation I listen to.

For example, I listen to exciting dance songs on my way to school and listen to ballad songs to concentrate when I study at school.

In addition, when I take a break at home, I listen to exciting songs and sing along to relieve my stress.

Like this, I enjoy listening to all genres of music. Below is the music playlist I've been listening to recently.
